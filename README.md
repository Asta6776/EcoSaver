# 🌍 EcoSaver
### *Track your energy usage, save the planet.*

> **🚀 Live Demo:** [Click here to try EcoSaver](https://asta6776.github.io/EcoSaver/)

![Screenshot of the app](icon-512.png) *(Note: You can replace this with a screenshot of your actual app interface)*

## 💡 The Problem
People lack simple, privacy-focused tools to monitor their daily digital energy footprint.

## ✅ The Solution
EcoSaver is a **Progressive Web App (PWA)** that runs entirely in your browser. It works offline, requires no login, and gives you instant feedback on your energy usage.

## 🛠️ Built With
* HTML5 & CSS3
* Vanilla JavaScript
* Service Worker (PWA) for Offline Support

## 🚀 How to use it
Just visit the [Live Demo](https://asta6776.github.io/EcoSaver/) above. No installation needed. You can even install it to your phone's home screen like a native app!
